About

Keshava Mansions is built on a site of 5,940 Sq. ft area with 11th Main Road to the East. The building has Basement, Ground, First and Second Floors, with a super built up area of 15,600 Sq. ft. It is constructed in accordance with BBMP regulations and is situated very close to Joy Allukas, GRT, Navarathan Jewellers. The present tenant is M/S Kalanjali Arts and Crafts, a Hyderabad based company.

Amenities

  • Lift - for up to 5 persons
  • Generator for entire building including lift
  • Sump tank 6,000 ltrs
  • Over head tank 6,000 ltrs
  • Borewell
  • Power supply up to 250 kVA
  • Car Parking - for up to 6 Vehicles
